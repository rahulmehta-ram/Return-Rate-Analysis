Finding the "Safe Harbor" in Indian E-commerce

Returns (RTO + Customer Returns) are the biggest profit killer in Indian e-commerce, especially on value platforms like Meesho. This interactive report analyzes which categories offer the lowest risk and highest stability.

Winner: Personal Care

Consistently the lowest return category (< 5%). Hygiene concerns make these non-returnable by policy on most platforms.

High Risk: Apparel

Highest returns (25-40%). Issues with fit, fabric feel, and "wardrobing" (wearing once and returning) drive this up.
